The Experience of Learning is a Shared One

Jam Sessionz is a concept for a mobile app that was created due to the struggles we were hearing in interviews from beginner music students on finding customized lesson plans and getting feedback.

    • Users desired feedback from an instructor
    • Users want to interact with other students
    • Users need a knowledgable instructor
