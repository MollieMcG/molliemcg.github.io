A music education app that fosters connection and growth.

The Experience of Learning is a Shared One

Jam Sessionz is a concept for a mobile app that was created due to the struggles we were hearing in interviews from beginner music students on finding customized lesson plans and getting feedback.

    • Users desired feedback from an instructor
    • Users want to interact with other students
    • Users need a knowledgable instructor

Step 1

We began with user interviews and a survey that gauged user’s level of experience with music, preferred learning style and what resources that currently use to learn new skills.

Step 2

Our competitive analysis consisted of five other music education apps. Each team member took detailed notes and went through a lesson plan to better list out strengths and pain points.

Step 3

With this information we began to brainstorm possible features for our app. After this brainstorm session we applied these to a creative matrix to sort out what was a must have deliverable for our deadline.

Features

Intro Survey

First time users of the app upon sign up have the option to complete a little survey that asks them about their level of experience, instruments they’re interested in and their goals. The purpose is to better suggest content and to encourage them via scheduled notifications.

Course Thumbnails

Each course thumbnail includes the amount of videos and the expected time to complete the course. In interviews user’s expressed wanting to see ratings from other students

Instructor Bio

A repeat comment during interviews was a desire for instructors with experience and proof of skill. With that in mind we wanted a little bio to highlight the instructor of the course.
