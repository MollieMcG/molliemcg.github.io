About

I’m Mollie McGalliard

I completed my BFA at UNC Charlotte in 2016.

After graduation I began working at Mom & Pop’s Print Shop. There I learned the in’s and out’s of print production, something I had no experience in, and I was determined to wear as many hats as possible. Working in the production manager role I was curious and excited to learn, with an eye for the finer details, quality control, and ready to go all hands in.

While looking for the next opportunity to learn and expand my skill set, I discovered the UNC Charlotte Bootcamp on UX/UI Design that I will complete in January of 2025. From there I’m looking to join a design team to work with other like minded creatives. I will bring the wealth of my experiences and energy to explore the future ahead!

Let’s chat and collaborate!
